# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

DeFi power users — experienced with CDPs, bonding curves, and yield strategies. They evaluate protocols critically, value technical depth, and can tell when a project is serious versus hype. They skim fast and want substance quickly.

## Product Purpose

Landing page for Polaris Finance, a DeFi protocol. The site's job is to establish credibility, explain the protocol's mechanics (pUSD stablecoin, pETH, POLAR governance token), and route serious users to the testnet/app and documentation. Success: a skeptical DeFi native leaves convinced the project is serious and clicks through to the app or docs.

## Brand Personality

Bold, visionary, warm. Ambitious but approachable — space exploration with a human touch. Polaris positions itself as a guiding star: confident in its direction, inviting others to follow. Authoritative without being cold, forward-looking without being vaporware.

## Anti-references

Generic DeFi aesthetics: neon accent gradients, rotating TVL counters, "launch app" urgency patterns, casino energy. Polaris should feel like discovering a constellation, not entering a casino.

## Design Principles

1. **Substance over spectacle.** Every visual element reinforces credibility. If it doesn't inform or orient, remove it.
2. **Warm precision.** Cream-on-navy warmth combined with precise spacing, alignment, and typographic hierarchy.
3. **Calm confidence.** Unhurried and assured. No urgency tactics, no visual noise.
4. **Celestial consistency.** Every component belongs in the same night sky — same palette, glass-like surfaces, subtle luminosity.
5. **Respect the reader.** Clear hierarchy, scannable sections, direct language over marketing fluff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Dark mode only. All motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`. Playwright + axe-core accessibility checks run in CI; Lighthouse audits enforce thresholds. Keep cream-on-navy text contrast high (per docs/design-system.md).
